Sesame Pasta Chicken Salad

Preparation

Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add sesame seeds, and cook stirring frequently until lightly toasted. Remove from heat, and set aside.

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta, and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente. Drain pasta, and rinse under cold water until cool. Transfer to a large bowl.

  2. In a jar with a tight-fitting lid, combine vegetable oil, soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, sesame seeds, ginger, and pepper. Shake well.

  3. Pour sesame dressing over pasta, and toss to coat evenly. Gently mix in chicken, cilantro, and green onions.

Ingredient List
1/4 cup of sesame seeds
1 (16 ounce) package of bow tie pasta
1/2 cup of vegetable oil
1/3 cup of light soy sauce
1/3 cup of rice vinegar
1 teaspoon of sesame oil
2 tablespoons of white sugar
1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
3 cups of shredded, cooked chicken breast meat
1/3 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
1/3 cup of chopped green onion
